Track 2: Iterative process

It is important to me to design something that is aesthetically pleasing and meets the needs of the user. To achieve this, during the process, I would constantly ask for feedback from classmates and teachers to help me with the design choices and if they are okay. If the design is not good, I would change it, of course. I would also like to know why it is not a great choice before moving on and changing it and recommendations on what I should do instead and how it is better.
