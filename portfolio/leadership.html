Track 3: Personal leadership

When I think of personal leadership I never really considered myself to be so much of a “personal leader”. But if I look back I am always looking for an opportunity to learn and grow as a person. I like to think that I have a lot of potential as a person so I want to make the most of myself. I am also very conscious of my choices and the consequences of my choices. I should maybe make some better choices… Anyways.

I don’t have a problem with receiving feedback, especially negative feedback. I learn more when I make mistakes and if my team members need to hold me accountable for something I will more than gladly listen and do my best to become a better team member.

In the pizzeria project I decided to take over almost all the tasks of the team members that did not show up. Which is okay, but I do need to learn how to work in a team instead of taking over people's tasks and doing it myself.
