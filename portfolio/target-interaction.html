Track 4: Target interaction

In my previous Pizzeria project. I learned how important it was to have good communication between your team and your client, meeting the needs of the stakeholder in a project, paying close attention to what they want and asking questions. Sometimes it can be hard for me to take the stakeholders into account because I want to go my own way with the project, my solution for that was to ask if my ideas were okay and if not I wipe it off the table. Now for my team, I failed to have good communication and the impact of that was very big. I did not address my team members about their behavior which in this case was their absence and we were left with an unfinished product. This time our group is very open and everyone will be held accountable for not doing their part and not being present. We made sure of that with our project plan.
